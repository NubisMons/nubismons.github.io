3. Convergence Theorem: $|f_n - f|_\infty \to 0 \iff f_n \to f$ uniformly a.e.

Proof Sketch:

  1. Given $|f_n - f|_\infty \to 0$. For each $m$, exists $A_m$ (null set) where error $> 1/m$.
  2. The Trick: Let $A = \bigcup_{m=1}^{\infty} A_m$.
    • $\mu(A) = 0$ (Countable union of null sets).
  3. On $A^c$: $|f_n - f| < 1/m$ for all large $n$. $\implies$ Uniform Convergence.
